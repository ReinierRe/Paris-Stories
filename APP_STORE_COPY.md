ABOUT THE APP:
City Stories is an AI-powered audio podcast app for travelers and culture enthusiasts. The app supports three European cities in v1.0: Amsterdam, Paris and Barcelona. Users can browse a curated library of topics per city, generate custom podcasts on any city-related subject, and listen with background playback. The app is fully free — no subscription, no in-app purchases.

HOW TO TEST:
1. Sign in with the demo account above.
2. You'll land on the Profile tab where Amsterdam and Paris are already activated under "My Cities". Tap "+ Add city" to download Barcelona (about 30 seconds).
3. Open the Library tab. Tap any city section header to expand it (Amsterdam is expanded by default). Tap a theme card to see topics. Tap a topic to start the customize flow.
4. Choose an angle, language, length, voice, and tap Create Podcast. Generation takes 30-60 seconds (this is expected — Anthropic Claude generates the script and Google TTS renders the audio).
5. Listen via the player screen. Test 15-second skip, background playback (lock screen), and transcript view.
6. Open the My Podcasts tab. Podcasts are grouped per city. Swipe left to delete.
7. To test the custom podcast flow: from any city library, tap "Add your own subject", type any topic, generate. Limit is 5 custom podcasts per account.

EXTERNAL SERVICES:
- Firebase Authentication — user accounts (email/password)
- PostgreSQL (https://paris-stories.replit.app) — podcast history, custom podcasts, city configs
- Anthropic Claude API — podcast script generation
- Google Cloud Text-to-Speech — audio narration
- Content moderation — Claude reviews every user-supplied topic before generation; rejects sexually explicit, violent or illegal content

PRIVACY POLICY:
https://paris-stories.replit.app/privacy-policy

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Podcast generation takes 30-60 seconds. This is expected AI processing time, not a bug.
- The app uses no location permission and no third-party IP. All audio is AI-generated by Google neural voices; all script content is original.
- DSA: registered as trader (Reinier Schneider, the Netherlands).
- Region: available worldwide.
- Per-city accounts: users have separate progression and podcast history per city (intentional design — being a "World Explorer" in Amsterdam is different from being one in Paris).
